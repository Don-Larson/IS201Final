A short intoduction

I'm Don Larson. I'm 22 and have lived most of life in Utah, except for 2 years I spent living in Taiwan, speaking Chinese, as a volunteer missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latterday Saints.

I enjoy anything with motors and/or wheels. I have grown up 4-wheeling and dirt biking. I love not only riding but working on atvs. I am a YouTube taught mechanic and love figuring out how things work and improving them.

Throughout my life I have been involved in my various family businesses. The first of which was making homemade bow and arrows and selling them at craft shows to ear money for a disney cruis. I love the innerworkings of businesses and it has been really cool to learn from my parents who are entrepreurs. Being involved in their busniness throughout my life has taught me about and motivated me to learn about how successful businesses are started and perpetuated.
