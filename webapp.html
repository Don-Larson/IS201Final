How this model works:
Estimated brake horsepower = stock crank horsepower × a boost-based power multiplier.
Estimated wheel horsepower = brake horsepower × (1 − drivetrain loss).
MPG change = small off-boost efficiency gain − larger penalty weighted by time in boost.
Engine life = baseline engine life ÷ adjusted stress (includes maintenance benefits).

The goal is to make the assumptions easy to explain and easy to edit for class. This version assumes a best-case stock WRX engine life baseline.
